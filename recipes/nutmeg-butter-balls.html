Nutmeg Butter Balls

Description

Nutmeg Butter Balls are sweet, bite-sized cookies made with butter, powdered sugar, and flour, flavored with a hint of nutmeg. They are rolled into small balls, baked until lightly golden, and then coated in more powdered sugar for a soft, melt-in-your-mouth texture. Perfect for holidays or special occasions!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup soft butter
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 1 and 1/2 cups shelled almonds
  • 2 cups flour, sifted
  • 1/2 cup powdered sugar
  • 2 teaspoons nutmeg

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 300 degrees
  2. Cream butter and sugar
  3. Add vanilla and almonds
  4. Put flour in with your fingers to make a smooth dough
  5. Shape into small balls and chill for a few hours
  6. Place on greased cookie sheets and bake 15 minutes
  7. Remove from oven and roll in to mixed nutmeg and sugar while still warm
