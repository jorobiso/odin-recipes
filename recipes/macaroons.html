Macaroons

Description

Macaroons are sweet, chewy cookies made from shredded coconut, sugar, and egg whites. They have a crispy exterior and a soft, moist center. Sometimes dipped in chocolate or flavored with vanilla or almond, theyâ€™re a delicious treat, often enjoyed during holidays or as a simple dessert.

Ingredients

  • 3 and 3/4 cups of powdered sugar
  • 2 and 2/3 cups of ground almonds
  • 7 egg whites
  • 1 teaspoon of backing powder

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 275 degrees
  2. Sift the sugar and mix with ground almonds
  3. Whip eggs stiff with baking powder
  4. Fold in the almond mixture until smooth
  5. Roll into small round balls and put each 1 inch apart from each other on greased cookie sheet covered with wax paper
  6. Bake for 20 minutes or until lightly browned
  7. Retrieve the cookies and invert the cookie sheet on a clean and flat surface
  8. Wet the back of the waxed paper with a cloth wrung out in very cold water, and they will all slip off easily
